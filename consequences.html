Why is Generative AI bad?



    Environmental Impacts
  • Data centers that run Generative AI use obsence amounts of power and water to run safely.
  • Generative AI being so "popular" has caused even more data centers to be built, which compounds the original problem.
  • Other resources that need to be mined for the electrical parts (Graphic Processing Units for example) are taxing our already over-tapped planet
  • People near where data centers are built and run are being asked to cut their own power/water demands to give more to the data centers.

    • Social Impacts
  • Generative AI models are trained on stolen data from all over the internet from artists to writers and everywhere in between.
  • Since Generative AI takes information from everywhere, it is very common for it to provide misleading or outright dangerous responses to people.
  • The previous in turn leads to people trusting information they shouldn't.
  • People are becoming so reliant on AI and generative AI in general that they are thinking less and less for themselves.
