Generative AI's drain on electricity





Generative AI is everywhere and doesn't seem to be loosing steam anytime soon. The strain on power is alarmingly high. With the national grid in the U.S. already struggling to keep up with user demand, Generative AI's power draw needs to be acknowledged.





MIT notes: "A U.S. Department of Energy study estimates that our data center's energy use, already at a record high, will at least double and may more than triple by 2028." While this may seem disheartening, there are some things you can do in order to do your part.
