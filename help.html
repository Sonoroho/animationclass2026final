What you can do to help.

  • Don't use any generative AI if at all possible
  • Avoid using/buying things that use generative AI
  • If you have to use it (for work or other reasons), then make sure to fact check everything it provides you with.
  • If you are an artist, make sure to use anti-scraping measures to keep your work safe.
  • If something has AI that doesn't need it, provide a bad review of the product and state why you are doing such.
  • Contact your local representatives to advocate for AI safety measures.
  • Fight local data center construction projects.
  • Stay informed of recent GenAI advancements as the more information you have, the more prepared you are.
